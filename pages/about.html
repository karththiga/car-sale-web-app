Company Introduction

LankaDrive Auto Sales (Pvt) Ltd is a Colombo-based automobile dealer focused on delivering dependable vehicles and customer-first service. We supply brand new imports, quality reconditioned vehicles from Japan, and carefully selected locally registered used vehicles.

To make vehicle ownership easy, transparent, and trustworthy for every Sri Lankan buyer.

Our Vision

  • Fast documentation and registration guidance.
